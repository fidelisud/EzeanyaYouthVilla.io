Who We Are

Ezeanya Youth Villa is the vibrant youth wing of Umuezeanya Quarter in Umuoba Anam community Anambra East LGA, Anambra State.We are committed to fostering unity, Empowerment, Education, skill acquisition and community Development among our young people. With passion and collective effort, Ezeanya Youth Villa continues to inspire positive change in Umuoba Anam at large

Our Mission

To empower,unite and uplift every youth of Umuezeanya Quarter by providing opportunities for personal growth, skill development,Education, leadership and community service while preserving our cultural heritage and contributing to the progress of our community

“Building today’s youth for a stronger Umuezeanya tomorrow”

Our Focus Areas

Skill Acquisition & Training

Workshops in digital skills, fashion, phone repair, agriculture and more

Education & Scholarship Support

Guidance, mentorship, and small grants for academic excellence

Community Development

Clean-up exercises, health campaigns, youth sports and infrastructure advocacy

Social & Cultural Events

End-of-year parties, cultural day, debates, and networking sessions

Empowerment & Welfare

Support for members in need, job referrals, business ideas

Entrepreneurship

Startup training, market linkage, mentorship and business incubation
